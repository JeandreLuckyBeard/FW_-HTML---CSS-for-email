We previously wrote to you to advise about changes to the risk scale we use to
measure and assess the risk within your portfolio.

This risk scale rates the possible level of risk, sometimes referred to as volatility, of
funds from Unio Rating UR1 to UR7. A fund rated UR1 will have a much lower level of risk
than a fund rated UR7. On 25/09/2023 we are updating the Unio Risk Scale used to
assess risk within your overall portfolio. This communication is for information purposes
only and you do not need to do anything as a result.

The Unio Risk Scale below summarises the volatility bands aligned to each risk rating.

It should be noted, fund providers generally use different risk scales for their fund
offerings. Funds managed by Irish Life Investment Managers (ILIM) and Setanta will
utilise the Unio Risk Scale. Other fund providers will continue to use their own rating
scale.

The updated Unio Risk Scale may mean a change to the risk rating number associated
with the funds you are invested in. There is no change to the underlying investments or
level of risk of your existing funds.
